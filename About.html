Veterinary clinic Mission :

The mission of veterinary clinic is to gather the veterinarian with animal owners to enhance the veterinary care by creating access to personalized ,vet specialty treatment at a reasonable cost across a virtual care platform

Veterinary clinic Story :

The animal community in Gaza Strip faces a shortage of veterinary specialists. With the shortage of supplies for the animal environment in Gaza Strip and the lack of university interest in providing the specialty of veterinary medicine, this has led to the shortage of veterinary specialists and many problems that affect animals and this may lead to the death of animals and the loss of animal owners their livelihood. Our veterinary clinic platform was born between these problems to find solutions The presence of our platform will solve these problems and alleviate the owners of animals through the ability to reach specialists to know the diseases of their animals and the most appropriate treatment for them. Because of our platform, there are no longer any limits to accessing specialists, nor for the services that the clinic can provide for the animals. Now the veterinary clinic is in its beginnings, but what is certain is that it will serve the Palestinian people in the Gaza Strip and animal owners in the future
